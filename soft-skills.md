# Continuous Development: Soft Skills

Soft skills should be developed throughout your embedded systems journey.

## Focus Areas

- Communication: explain technical trade-offs clearly to teams and stakeholders.
- Problem-solving: break complex failures into testable hypotheses.
- Teamwork: collaborate across firmware, hardware, and testing roles.
- Documentation: write concise design notes, bug reports, and bring-up logs.
- Professional ownership: follow through from issue discovery to resolution.

## How to Practice Continuously

- Do post-mortems after bugs or failed bring-up attempts.
- Present your design decisions in short weekly updates.
- Pair with teammates during debugging sessions.
- Track decisions in a personal engineering journal.
