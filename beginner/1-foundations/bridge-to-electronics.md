# Bridge to Electronics Fundamentals

After calculus and circuit laws, the next step is component-level understanding. These parts are the building blocks of embedded hardware.

## Major Components to Master

### Resistors

- Used for current limiting.
- Used for voltage division and signal conditioning.

### Capacitors

- Used for noise filtering and decoupling.
- Used in timing and transient response control.

### Diodes and Transistors

- Used for direction control, protection, and switching.
- Used to drive loads that a microcontroller cannot power directly, such as relays or motors.

## What to Do Next

Move from theory into hands-on prototyping:

- Build simple circuits on a breadboard.
- Validate calculations with a multimeter.
- Iterate by comparing expected and measured behavior.
